$x$ = Point x on ellipse.  
$y$ = Point y on ellipse.  
$v$ = Height of ellipse.  
$h$ = Width of ellipse.  
$t$ = Angle of ellipse.  
$m$ = Slope of line.  
$a_1$ = x-intercept.  
$b_1$ = y-intercept.  
$y=mx+b_1$  
$\frac{x}{a_1}+\frac{y}{b_1} = 1$  
$y-y_1=m(x-x_1)$  

$v^{2}x^{2}\cos(t)^{2}+2v^{2}x(mx+b_1)\cos(t)\sin(t)+v^{2}(mx+b_1)^{2}\sin(t)^{2}+h^{2}(mx+b_1)^{2}\cos(t)^2-2h^{2}x(mx+b_1)\cos(t)\sin(t)+h^{2}x^{2}\sin(t)^{2}-h^{2}v^{2}=0$
